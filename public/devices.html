About Device Management

This page shows all WebSocket connections, including hardware devices like starters and lane timers from the swimwatch-hardware project.

Device Registration: Devices automatically register when they connect and send their MAC address, IP, role, and lane number.

Configuration: You can update the role (starter/lane) and lane number for any registered device.

Status: The table shows connection status and the last time each device was seen.
